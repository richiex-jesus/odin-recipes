How To Make a Delicious Cheeseburger (with a little cheese)

Ingredients and Substitutions:

  • Ground Beef (85/15 or 80/20, sometimes 90/10)
  • Secret Ingredients (olive oil + Worecestershire sauce)
  • Seasonings (sea salt, black pepper, garlic powder)

How To Make Juicy Burgers:

  1. Mix the beef, oil, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, salt and pepper. Work through the mixture using your hands until just combined.
  2. Shape the burger patties. Make 1/2 inch-think patties. Make a thumb print in the center of each patty.
  3. Grill or pan fry. Preheat your grill over medium-high heat. If you're using the stovetop, preheat a cast iron skillet or grill pan on the stove instead. Cook burgers until browned on the bottom. Flip, add cheese if you want, and cook until done to your liking.
  4. Rest. Remove from heat. Let your homemade burgers rest for a few minutes before serving, which lets the juices settle.
